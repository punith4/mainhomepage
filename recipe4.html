cheese chicken

Ingredients: chicken,cheese,broccoli,salt,pepper and flour mixture. Making: 1. Slice the chicken into 3 thinner slices. Sprinkle lightly with salt/pepper and Italian seasoning. 2. Dredge in flour mixture. Sear in olive oil for 4-5 minutes per side, until a golden crust has developed. Set aside. 3. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Add the flour and cook for 2 more minutes. 4.Add the chicken broth in small splashes, stirring continuously. Add the milk in the same manner. Add the seasonings, hot sauce, and broccoli. 5. cook the broccoli for 4-5 minutes. Reduce heat to low. Your dish is ready.
